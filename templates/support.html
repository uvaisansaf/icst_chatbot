Frequently Asked Questions

How do I retrain the chatbot model?

Go to Dashboard and click the "Retrain Model" button. The training process completes instantly — no ML training needed. The fuzzy-difflib engine reloads all active intents immediately.

What file formats are supported for export?

You can export data in JSON, CSV, or Excel format. The export includes all conversations, intents, patterns, responses, and analytics data.

How can I add new intents?

Navigate to "Q&A Library" and click "Add New Intent". Enter a unique tag, display name, and description. Then add questions (patterns) and answers (responses) to complete the intent.

Why are some responses not matching correctly?

The model uses a confidence threshold (default 70%). If confidence is low, it falls back to pattern matching. Add more question variations for the intent or adjust the confidence threshold in Settings.

How do I import the ICST FAQ database?

Click the "Import All FAQs" button on the Dashboard. This will automatically create intents for all pre-defined ICST University questions and answers (over 500 entries).

Can I customize the chatbot's appearance?

Yes! Go to Settings → Appearance to change theme colors, enable dark mode, and adjust font sizes. All settings are saved to your session.
